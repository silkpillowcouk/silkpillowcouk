What are the benefits of using silk pillowcases?

Silk pillowcases offer several benefits, including reduced friction on the skin and hair, which helps to minimize frizz and breakage. They also help to maintain skin hydration and are less likely to absorb moisture from your skin compared to cotton pillowcases.

How do I know if a silk pillowcase is real silk?

To determine if a pillowcase is real silk, check for a label that specifies "100% silk" or "pure silk." You can also look for the momme weight; higher momme counts (between 19-25) indicate better quality silk. Additionally, real silk has a smooth, soft texture and a natural sheen.

Can silk pillowcases help with acne?

Yes, silk pillowcases can help with acne as they are less absorbent than cotton, meaning they retain less oil and bacteria. This can help reduce breakouts and create a healthier sleeping environment for your skin.

How do I care for silk pillowcases?

Silk pillowcases should be hand washed or machine washed on a gentle cycle using cold water and a mild detergent. Air drying is recommended, and avoid using bleach or fabric softeners. Check the care label for specific instructions, as some silk pillowcases may have different care requirements.
