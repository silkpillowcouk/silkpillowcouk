Care Instructions for Blissy Pillowcases

To maintain the luxurious feel and quality of Blissy pillowcases, proper care is essential. Washing them in cold water using a gentle cycle is recommended. Avoid harsh detergents that can affect the silk. It's best to use a mild detergent specifically designed for silk fabrics to prevent any damage. Refrain from using bleach or fabric softeners as these can degrade the material over time.

When it comes to drying, air drying is ideal. Placing the pillowcases flat on a clean surface helps maintain their shape and prevents wrinkles. If you must use a dryer, select a low setting and remove them while slightly damp to minimize heat exposure. Storing them in a cool, dry place will further ensure their longevity and softness, keeping your investment in premium sleep quality intact.

How to Maintain Quality

To preserve the luxurious feel and sheen of Blissy pillowcases, proper laundering techniques are essential. Always check the care label for specific washing instructions. Washing with cold water is recommended to prevent shrinkage and fading. Using a delicate cycle on the washing machine can help minimize agitation, which might otherwise cause wear and tear over time.

Drying is also a crucial step in maintaining quality. Air-drying your pillowcases is the best option, as it prevents the heat from a dryer from damaging the fabric. If you must use a dryer, opt for the lowest heat setting to avoid any potential harm. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to avoid any discoloration. Regularly checking for any signs of wear will help ensure your pillowcases remain in excellent condition for a longer period.
